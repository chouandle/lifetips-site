Quick Fixes

60-Second Life Hacks

Short, actionable tips you can try right now to save time and energy.

💡

Smelly Sneakers?

Place dry tea bags inside your shoes overnight to absorb moisture and unpleasant odors naturally.

🍋

Microwave Steam Clean

Microwave a bowl of water with lemon slices for 3 minutes. The steam loosens all the gunk!

🍌

Ripen Bananas Fast

Need ripe bananas? Put them in the oven at 150°C for 15–30 mins until the skins turn black.

🔋

Battery Check Trick

Drop a battery on a table. If it bounces once and falls, it's full. Multiple bounces? It's empty.
